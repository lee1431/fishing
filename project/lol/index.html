Manifesto

1) Stories are welcome.

But your position must be explainable without your feelings. If it can’t be tested, it can’t be traded.

2) Execution beats prophecy.

We don’t predict. We react with structure. Confirmation earns conviction.

3) Memes are medicine.

Drip is how we stay humble. If you can’t joke, you’re probably over-levered.

4) Survivors get the real big moves.

Big shorts don’t come from belief. They come from being there when liquidity breaks.

Rules (simple & stupid)

A) Size is sacred.

Trade size you can laugh through. If you can’t laugh, you sized wrong.

B) Receipts or it didn’t happen.

Post entries/exits when possible. We’re here for clarity, not mythology.

C) No guru worship.

We bow to liquidity, not influencers. Question everything (especially yourself).

D) Be kind, be funny.

People lose money. Don’t pile on. Drip on the trade, not the trader.
